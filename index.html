Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, you can do so directly within the Svogo mobile application. Go to the settings menu, select "Account," and then choose "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your personal information from our servers, except for essential information required to maintain our system integrity, such as your email address and login credentials (e.g., Apple ID) for re-login purposes. Please note that once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone, and your data will be irreversibly removed. If you have any issues or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at svogoapp@gmail.com.
